PictureSlides

What is PictureSlides?

PictureSlides is a plugin to DOMAssistant, and it is a highly customizable JavaScript-based way to easily turn your images into a collection viewable as a slideshow, and with fading effects, if desired. It can be a stand-alone slideshow or have a complete image library look with thumbnails, navigaiton buttons etc.
